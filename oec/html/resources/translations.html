Translations

The goal of the OEC is to become an online resource for global trade. As such we would like to attract as many international visitors to the site as possible. The goal of translating the site is two-fold, part 1 being to "translate" the official esoteric HS and SITC product classification names and part 2 being to translate these cleaned up names from english to the other 15 languages supported by the site.

How to Help

Are you a bilingual speaker with some free time on your hands? If so, then you can help us improve our translations on the site. Have we mentioned that the site and all of its data are open source? By lending a hand, you can help to create a global resource for others wishing to work with international trade data.

Instructions

Below you'll see headers for each language supported by the site with links to a Google Spreadsheet that holds the relevant translations. For each of the products listed in the spreadsheet there are "Name", "Description", "Keyword", "Gender", "Plural" and "Article" columns. Right-click on the cell you wish to edit and click Insert Comment.

Name
The short name which will appear in all of the visualizations (please keep this short as space is at a premium in all data visualizations)
Description
This is the longer definition of the product used to give a more in-dpeth description of how this product differs from another that might be similar.
Keywords
These are synonyms that could be used for better search results e.g. "auto, vehicle, truck" for the product "Cars". Please enter these as a comma separated list.
Gender
If the name supplied takes a gender in the language you are contributing. Please use "m" for masculine and "f" for feminine.
Plural
Whether or not the name supplied is plural. Please use 1 to indicate yes and 0 or empty cell to indicate no.
Article
Whether or not the name supplied requires an article i.e. "the". Please use 1 to indicate yes and 0 or empty cell to indicate no.
